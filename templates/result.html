Health Recommendations

What To Do

  • Exercise regularly - at least 30 minutes of moderate activity most days
  • Maintain a healthy balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables and whole grains
  • Monitor and control your blood pressure regularly
  • Keep your blood sugar and cholesterol levels in check
  • Take prescribed medications as directed by your doctor
  • Get adequate sleep (7-8 hours per night)
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water
  • Manage stress through relaxation techniques
  • Have regular medical check-ups

What To Avoid

  • Smoking or exposure to secondhand smoke
  • Excessive alcohol consumption
  • Foods high in saturated fats and trans fats
  • High sodium/salt intake
  • Sedentary lifestyle with prolonged inactivity
  • Excessive stress and anxiety
  • Skipping medications or medical appointments
  • Processed foods and sugary beverages
  • Irregular eating patterns

Important Note

If you experience any of these stroke warning signs, seek immediate medical attention:

  • Sudden numbness or weakness in face, arm, or leg
  • Sudden confusion or trouble speaking
  • Sudden vision problems
  • Sudden trouble walking or dizziness
  • Sudden severe headache
